About This Application

This interactive 3D molecular visualization tool is designed specifically for CBSE Class 12 chemistry students. It includes all the important organic compounds from the syllabus, allowing you to:

  • View 3D molecular structures with realistic bond angles and lengths
  • Rotate and zoom to examine molecules from different angles
  • Understand spatial arrangements of atoms in organic compounds
  • Compare different functional groups and their structures

Built with Flask, RDKit for molecular generation, and 3Dmol.js for visualization.
